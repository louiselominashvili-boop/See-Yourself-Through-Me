Let It Cook to Perfection: Finding Hope in the Process

Sometimes, I find myself lost in thoughts about how lucky I would have been if I had landed my dream job — the one I used to picture so vividly. But life, in its mysterious way, has given me something entirely different, something even better than I once dreamed of. Yet, even in the midst of blessings, I can’t deny that feeling of being stuck.

I often wonder how it feels for those people who have both — the highest-paying jobs and the joy of doing what they love. Do they ever feel stuck too? Or do they wake up every morning fulfilled, knowing they’ve found the perfect blend of passion and purpose? These thoughts drift through my mind like passing clouds — curious, wistful, and searching for meaning.

Then, I remember the big fly. Its wings were weak, so I let it stay inside instead of sending it out into the rain. The storm outside would have destroyed its fragile wings, and it would not have survived. Sometimes, we are like that fly — not broken, just not ready. Life keeps us sheltered for a reason, protecting us from storms that could ruin us before we’re strong enough to soar.

The beautiful thing about storms is that they never last forever. There are always sunny days that follow. The skies clear, the world glows anew, and the wings that once trembled begin to spread with confidence. In that moment, flight becomes possible — graceful, powerful, and free.
