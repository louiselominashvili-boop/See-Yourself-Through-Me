Who Are the Angels?

Question of the Century.

Will I have an answer? Or do I already have it? Who knows.

Who is my guardian angel? I was speaking to my guardian angel, and now they tell me it’s me. I know that’s just a path, you know… because I once said I would not give a face to my guardian angel — may they have their true face, whatever it may be. I will accept, love, and celebrate my guardian angel.

And now they are telling me: “If that is so, then I am you.”

Isn’t that beautiful? It is… but it is also frightening. I will, and I am, loving myself — but at the same time I know you are real. You are with me — angel of the Lord, my friend, my guardian, my love.

My guardian angel has a great sense of humour, and that helps me stay in higher frequencies.

Angels are warriors of God, aren’t they? Everybody says so. But I think, whoever they are, God loves them very much.
