The End

Sometimes, you believe you are deeply in love, living in perfect harmony with another person. It feels so complete that the idea of an ending seems impossible. Yet the moment the question “How could this ever end?” enters your conscious mind, something important has already shifted.

That question is not random. It rises from the unconscious, which often understands the truth before we are ready to accept it. By the time doubt reaches awareness, the unconscious has already sensed the distance, the silence, or the emotional separation. In that quiet place beneath thought, the relationship has already begun to loosen its grip.

What feels sudden to the conscious mind is, in reality, a long, silent realization. The unconscious knows first. And when it speaks, it is usually because the ending has already arrived.
